Vision & Mission

Vision

The creation of school personnel who are faithful and pious, innovative, with character through learning communities to increase global competitiveness.

Mission

  1. 1. Cultivate a school life that is religious and has noble character in accordance with their respective religions and beliefs.
  2. 2. Exploring the potential of human resources and developing them in accordance with technological developments.
  3. 3. Improving discipline for school residents.
  4. 4. Creating a superior and competitive school in the academic and non-academic fields.
  5. 5. Realizing education that prioritizes lifelong learning by involving all school members and parents of students.
